ImageMeasurer OSS

Upload an image, set one known length, then draw as many lines as you want.

No backend. No cookies. No tracking. Fully local.

Load an image to start.

Tutorial

  • 1) Upload your image.
  • 2) Enter the known value and unit (example: 4.5 cm).
  • 3) Click Apply known length.
  • 4) Draw your first line on the known segment (reference).
  • 5) Draw more lines to measure other segments.
  • 6) Hover a line to edit it with draggable handles.
  • 7) Click a handle once to select it and fine-tune with arrow keys.
  • 8) Hold Shift + arrows to move 10 px per step.
  • 9) Double click any line to remove it.
  • 10) Export your results as CSV, TXT or PNG.
